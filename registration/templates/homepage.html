Understanding Your Data Protection Role

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITY

Data Controller

The decision-maker who determines the purpose and means of processing personal data.

  • Determines what data is collected
  • Defines how it will be processed
  • Establishes why it's needed

Key Responsibilities

  • Register with POTRAZ
  • Conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments
  • Implement appropriate security measures
  • Respond to data subject requests
Real-World Example

A bank collecting customer information for account opening is the Controller of that data.

vs
SECONDARY ROLE

Data Processor

The service provider that processes data on behalf of the Controller.

  • Follows Controller's instructions
  • Has no purpose of its own
  • Bound by contract

Key Responsibilities

  • Only process as instructed
  • Maintain processing records
  • Implement security measures
  • Report breaches within 72 hours
Real-World Example

A cloud storage provider hosting customer data for a bank is the Processor.

Comparative Summary

Aspect Data Controller Data Processor
Decision Making Determines purposes and means of processing Follows controller's instructions
Legal Liability Primary liability for compliance Liable only for processor-specific obligations
Relationship Engages processors via contracts Cannot engage sub-processors without authorization
Registration Must register with POTRAZ Registration may be required depending on activities
